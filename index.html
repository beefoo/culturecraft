A screen recording of CultureCraft app that has a pointer brushing cut-out pieces of visual art onto a canvas

CultureCraft is a free online tool for making intricate collages using open access art and imagery from prominent libraries and museums across the country.

Featured collections

  • Open Access at The Met

    The Met is the most popular art museum in the United States.

  • Smithsonian Open Access

    The Smithsonian Institution is the world's largest museum, education, and research complex.

  • NYPL Public Domain Collections

    The New York Public Library is the second largest public library in the United States.

  • Biodiversity Heritage Library

    The Biodiversity Heritage Library is the world's largest open access digital library for biodiversity literature and archives.

  • NASA Image and Video Library

    The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is the global leader in space exploration.

  • Citizen DJ at the Library of Congress

    Audio provided by Citizen DJ at the Library of Congress, the largest library in the world.
